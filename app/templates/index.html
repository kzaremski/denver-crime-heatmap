Denver Crime Heatmap


Choose a heatmap variant to display: Combined shows a blend of crime likelihood and types, Crime Likelihood highlights areas with higher predicted crime rates, and Crime Type focuses on the distribution of different crime categories.
Move the slider to view past, present, and future predictions up to 7 days into the future.
Click the button to automatically center the map on your current location. This helps you quickly view crime data for the area around you.
Click the button to center the map on downtown Denver.
